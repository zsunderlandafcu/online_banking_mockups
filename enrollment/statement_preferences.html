Online Statement Preferences

Congratulations on election to receive your statements online. Click on the "Confirm" button to confirm your preference of viewing your financial information exclusively online.

By viewing your statement information online, you'll benefit by:

  • Having access to your online statement anytime day or night.
  • Helping the environment by consuming less paper.
  • Leaving yourself less vulnerable to identity theft from mailed statements.

By clicking "Confirm" below, you agree to accept online through the Online Banking system the following statement documents: (1) periodic account statements, (2) annual notice of billing error rights, (3) annual privacy notice, (4) annual Electronic Funds Transfer Notice, and (5) year-end IRS 1098, 1099 and other applicable tax statement. You may access your statements as PDF documents through the Online Banking system. You must have Adobe Acrobat ReaderTM software to access the statements and a printer or ability to download the statements for you records. The statements for the new month will be available on the 5th of each month.

You have the right to request and receive periodic statements in paper form at any time or you may withdraw your consent to receive online statements by contacting any branch office, by calling (800) 999-3961 or by sending a secure message. There are no fees or account restrictions for choosing to withdraw your consent for online statements or to request a paper statement.
